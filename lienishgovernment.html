    Government of Lienia!

    Lienish Gdarames

    The legislative branch in Lienia is Parliament, which we call Gdarames. Now our Prime Minister is Ludmila T. What Gdarames does:

    • make new laws
    • represents citizens in government

    Lienish President

    The executive branch in Lienia is President. Now our President is Mikhail Nieviezhyn, which was chosen in election in 2022. The election happening once in four years. What Lienish President does:

    • commands the military
    • endorse or doesn't endorse laws

    Lienish Laws

    Lienia is unitary republic - it means what in all country same laws, courts, constitution etc. Lienia has constitution, which has been written by President at 25th of October in 2021. Now Lienia has these laws:

    • Lienish Constitution
    • Law about Lienish Currency
    • Law about Lienish Military Neutrality

    How make new laws in Lienia? First Gdarames (or, rarely, President) makes a prototype of new law, in which s\he writes its basic conceptions. Then Gdarames reads it and votes for this law, and if more than 50% agree - the law is passed. Then President endorse or doesn't endorse the law - if he endorsed it, law become valid.

    Lienish Diplomatic Policy

    Diplomatic relations is very important step in micronationalism for every country. We endorse diplomatic contacts with another micronations - you can write e-mail to our Foreign Secretary. Our relations can begin only if your country keep our conditions. If any of these conditions are not met our diplomatic relations will end. While you writing a letter, please specify:

    • name of the country
    • its political system
    • its website

      Our full list of conditions:

    • Nation has a documented existence of at least 3 months
    • Nation holds no laws of a racist or hateful nature
    • Nation's political system is not:
      • fascism
      • communism
      • theocracy
      • or absolute monarchy
    • Nation keep basic human rights
    • Nation don't talk lies of its history or politic
    • President (King, Emperor etc.) and/or Foreign Secretary should now about this contact
