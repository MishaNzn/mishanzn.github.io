Culture of Lienia!

Lienish Cuisine

Our main national dish is "kotsoopkies" - it is small pancakes, which cooking from kefir, egg, flour, sugar, butter and baking soda (we cook them sometimes - they are pretty good). Our next national dish will be a potato buns (this we've not cooked yet) - it will be buns with smashed potato in its dough. It also will be stuffed with mushrooms and cheese (in my opinion it will be pretty good).

Lienish Clothes

Lienish everyday clothes is simple: black or dark-blue jeans and thin sweater (climate in Lienia is little cold) predominantly sunny (yellow, orange, ocher etc.) colors.

Lienio Sperhe (Lienish Language)

Lienia has its very own language - Lienish. It's history begun at 25th, October - it's the date, when our President begun create book about Lienish language and begun translate our Constitution on Lienish. At 30th of October he began create Lienish-Russian Dictionary. First book on Lienish he began translate at 5th January 2022. It was "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone".
