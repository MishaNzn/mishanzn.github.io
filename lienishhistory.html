    History of Lienia!

    Republic of Hwamba

    Campaign of creating our micronation begun on 26th of November in 2019 year. It was the birthday of our President, Mikhail N. His Mom's friend granted him a book How to Build Your Own Country by Valery Wyatt. This book was about micronations and how create them. First attempt of creating micronation was on 4th of December in 2019 year. That micronation's name was Hwamba. Unfortunately, Hwamba was very weak and simple nation. it didn't have any money, constitution and laws. Only president and some territories.

    Creating of Lienia

    Unfortunately Hwamba's destiny was sad - it has broken after year of existing because of lack of government's control (but micronations often become weak or die because of this reason). Then Mikhail realized, what he needs to create idea of country (like its constitution and money), and just after this create country. His country name was Lienia. He didn't hurry with creating of Lienia. He remembered the Hwambian experience, so first, he created all government etc., and just then created Lienia. Actually, Lienia become independent in 22nd of January in 2022, but its constitution was written three months earlier!
