    History of Lienia!

    Republic of Hwamba

    Campaign of creating of micronation begun on 26th of November in 2021 year. It was the birthday of our President, Mikhail N. His Mom's friend present him a book How to Build Your Own Country by Valery Wyatt. This book was about micronations and how create them. First attempt of creating micronation was on 4th of December in 2019 year. That micronation's name was Hwamba.
