    History of Lienia!

    Republic of Hwamba

    Campaign of creating of micronations begun on 26th of November in 2021 year. It was the birthday of our President, Mikhail N. His Mom's friend granted him a book How to Build Your Own Country by Valery Wyatt. This book was about micronations and how create them. First attempt of creating micronation was on 4th of December in 2019 year. That micronation's name was Hwamba. Unfortunately, Hwamba was very weak and simple nation. She didn't have any money, constitution and laws. Only president and some territories.

    Creating of Lienia

    Unfortunately Hwamba's destiny was sad - it has broken after year of existing because of lack of government's control. Then Mikhail realized, what you need to create idea of country (like its constitution and money), and just after this create country. His country name was Lienia. He didn't hurry with creating of Lienia. He remembered the Hwambian experience, so first, he created all government etc., and just then created Lienia. Unfortunately, now Lienia is a president republic until it get full independence (technically, Lienia still dependence until Act of Independence).
